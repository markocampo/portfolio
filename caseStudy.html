Integrating 'free media' into Dissolve's business model

Dissolve â€“ Stock footage and photography

Dissolve Inc. is an online marketplace selling stock footage and photography. Users are typically creative nomads, production studios, and anyone that uses digital media in their personal or commercial projects.

Fierce competition

Competitors undercut prices and are expanding into other online services to attract a broader audience.

Additionaly, free media is disrupting the industry. Uninformed creatives flock to online platforms that allow anyone to use their media assets for free, hurting sales and directing traffic away from Dissolve.

Product strategy

The goal is to reinforce Dissolve's user base and digital media library by incorporating free media into its business model and capturing the associated audience.

The strategy is to empower creatives by giving them the ability to:

  • Upload digital media and permit anyone to use them for free
  • Download free media and use them in their projects
  • Monitize original content using Dissolve's contributor process
  • Facillitate the sharing and discovery of their media assets

Simultaneously, we'll re-engage Dissolve's existing users by integrating them into these new functionalities.

Minimum viable product

The MVP focuses on giving users the ability to upload, discover, and engage.

Requirements:

  1. User profile pages
  2. Upload interface
  3. Content detail page
  4. Content engagement and metrics
  5. Discoverability

Reverse engineering existing solutions

I deconstructed successful applications that already have years of user research and data integrated into their user experience. This process allowed me to identify key product components and gain valuable insight into proven UI and UX strategies, which ultimately influenced design decisions. Instagram, Unsplash, and Pexels were prime inspirators.

User profile page

Below I identified the main components in Unsplash's profile page:

  1. Upload functionality in the navigation bar
  2. User details section
  3. Content tabbing
  4. Content displayed in a grid view

Adapting the research to Dissolve's product requirements and constraints

  • Enable users to display their job title and location. This information is highly saught after in the media industry.
  • Allow users to display their personal website and social media accounts.
  • Enable users to follow each other.
  • Integrate Dissolve's existing users(contributors) and their stock products.
  • Implement the upload functionality in the profile page instead of the navigation bar in the interim due to technological constraints.

Accomodating all user types

The challenge was making the profile page useful for 3 distinct users:

Creatives

  • Submits media available for free download
  • Potentially wants to monitize their digital assets

Contributors

  • Submits media for sale
  • Potentially wants to provide free media samples

Consumers

  • Uses digital media in their projects
  • Interacts with digital content

Implementing tabs in the profile page will allow users to quickly switch between free and paid content without interupting their workflow.

Uploading digital content

The upload tool lets users submit multiple files at a time. They can attach metadata to improve SEO on online search engines.

To encourage users to review their submissions and attach metadata, users cannot complete the upload process until they view all of their uploads.

Ideally, we want users making their content available for free download, so the checkbox to enable this is on by default.

Using machine learning to enhance the user experience

Image recognition is a feature in the upload tool that automatically generates keywords for uploaded content. If users dislike the generated keywords, they can add their own custom keywords.

This software was implemented to help users speed up their workflow and to produce accurate results when consumers search the site for specific content.

Detecting sensitive content

The image recognition software is equipped to detect and blur sensitive content, but users will still have the ability to view them at their own discretion.

Content moderators monitor sensitive content in the admin page. They can approve false-positives which removes the blurred filter, or permenantly delete content if it breaches Dissolve's upload terms.

Integrating contributors and their digital products

The stock tab lets contributors display their digital products on their profile page, and also exposes the new creative audience to the idea of monitizing their digital assets.

Challenges integrating contributors

  1. The contributor site where contributors upload stock media is hosted on a seperate server than the commerce site.
  2. Profile pages are accessed from the commerce site, and are only associated with commerce accounts.
  3. Stock media is only associated with contributor accounts.
  4. Contributor and commerce accounts are not associated with each other.
  5. Not every contributor has a commerce account because they are mainly used by customers to purchase stock.

Connecting digital products

The 'Connect products' tool is an interim solution for the challenges outlined above. This allows contributors who own commerce accounts manually connect their stock products to their profile page.

The first step prompts contributors to search for their 'Alias' in Dissolve's database.

Next, contributors can review and confirm the resulting product library.

If the contributor surfaced the wrong library, they can search again by clicking the back button.

Once a contributor confirms their product library, they must verify themselves by logging into their contributor account.

The contributor's products will be displayed in the stock tab if they successfully logged in.

Interacting with digital content

Users can interact with content thumbnails accross the site by hovering over them.

Initial state

Videos will have a time duration stamp in the corner, not only to let users know how long a video is, but also to determine whether content is a photo or a video.

Hover state

On hover, users access primary actions tailored to consumers that enable them to like, share, download, and add to a collection.

Users can navigate to the owners profile by clicking their display name or avatar.

3 dot menu

These secondary actions are tailored to specific users. These include content owners, who wish to edit or delete their content, or view their content metrics in the info panel.

Dissolve selects is only viewable by Dissolve administrators and is used to feature content on marketing pages.

Viewing content in detail

The single page view is where users are directed when they click on a media thumbnail, or get shared content from Dissolve. It allows users to focus on individual uploads, allowing them to view enlarged versions, leave comments, and access the same user controls when hovering over the thumbnail version.

Additonaly, users can seamlessly discover related content if the one in focus does not meet their requirements. There are 2 ways of diverging, clicking media thumbnails under the similar content section, or clicking related keywords in the keywords section.

Discovering profiles and media content

Users will be able to search for specific profiles using the existing search field in the navigation bar.

The profile index page features both contributor and creator profiles. Dissolve's contributors are the bread and butter of it's business, so the first 4 rows exclusively displays contributor profiles.

Developer hand-off and project management

Being involved throughout the development process is essential when implementing these new functionalities. There are many moving parts, and careful planning and oversight is required to help the development team turn high-fidelity designs into real functional features.

At the end of the design process, I use Trello project management system to document design instructions, plan development sprints, and assign and deligate projects to developers. I employ an agile project management strategy in order to quickly deploy and iterate new features.
