Company Background

Dissolve Inc. is an online marketplace selling stock footage and photography servicing 2 specific users:

  1. Contributors who license and sell stock media
  2. Clients who purchase stock media

Dissolve seeks to service a brand new user, the Creator. The creator is anyone who hosts media assets online.

Doing business is hard if you don't have the right tools

There are websites that allow it's users to host media assetsâ€”everything their users upload becomes free for the public to use. Unfortunately, these websites don't have the systems in place that allow creatives to profit off their media assets.

Empowering the creator

Dissolve Creators™ gives the power back to the creator. You decide if you want to make your media assets downloadable for free, or if you want to put them up for sale. Here's how I designed it.

Reverse engineering competitor products

I started my research by analysing competitor software. Deconstructing existing platforms allowed me to identify the main components. This method gives me insight on proven strategies, saving me time from figuring out the basics, and allowing me to focus on innovating and implementing our solution.

Below are the requirements for our minimum viable product:

Product Requirements

  1. User profile pages
  2. Upload interface
  3. Monitization interface
  4. Content engagement and metrics
  5. Communications
  6. Discoverability

Business Requirements

  1. Integrate Dissolve's commerical licensing solution
  2. Integrate Dissolve's existing user base
  3. Implement a subscription system to monitize
  4. Merge Dissolve's premium stock products with free media assets submitted by creators

Working in an agile environment allows our team to scope in additional requirements as we develop the application.


Iterating through designs

The core: User Profile pages

The challenge: Designing the profile page so that it's useful for all 3 of Dissolve's user types.

Implementing a tabular structure to accomodate all 3 users.

In order to understand the users, I'll have to empathize with them and take on their persona.

As a consumer, I want to find the most information about the user here. I want to indulge in their content, follow them, and be able to reach out if I want to purchase media they didn't list for free.

As a creator, it's where I can upload fresh content and choose if I want to make them available for free, or for portfolio purposes only.

I can easily licence my content and generate income from my passion.

If I want to sell my media assets in bulk, I'll have to become a Dissolve Contributor. As a contributor I can automatically price my media in batches and turn my profile page into a store!

Upload feature

Creators will need a way to get their media assets online. Our upload process allows creators to attach metadata to their content empowering them to increase relevency in our search engine.

Creators can decide if they want to make their media assets downloadable for free, or for portfolio purposes only.
